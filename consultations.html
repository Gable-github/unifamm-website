Consultations with us.

For every consultation, we employ a tried and tested workflow which has greatly helped all of our customers.

Our workflow encompasses 3 actions - Research, Consultancy & Application.

Research

We provide clients with the latest food ingredients, handling techniques, as well as methods of food processing through research and innovation.

Consultancy

We equipped ourselves with the right skills to appropriately advise clients on any enquiries regarding food ingredients, manufacturing processes and applications. We are able to assist you in tackling any problems faced during food manufacturing or when handling specialty food ingredients.

Application

We constantly work on new innovative ways of incorporating new ingredients into existing food products. Whether it is new recipe formulations or enhancements, Unifamm Trading is here to help you achieve your manufacturing and processing needs.
